Target Audience

Description

The target audience of this site are those looking to participate in outdoor activities. This will most likely be people 12 and older. I would anticipate the main audience to be parents who may be planning a family outing or young adults planning an outdoor get-together.

Personas

Tony

Tony is a college student who is also a family home evening group leader. He often needs to check the weather to see what kinds of activities might be good idea for family home evening.

Pepper

Pepper is a wife and mother who loves to plan picnics for her family. She always checks the weather ahead of time to make sure she plans accordingly.

Scenarios

1) Natasha wants to have a picnic with her family. She wants to know if it will be too windy.

2) Steve loves to play soccer with his buds. It's hard to play in the rain so he checks online to see the forecast after class.

3) Sharon decides she wants to have a photoshoot outside with he friends. She doesn't want the wind and humidity to be an issue so she goes to the web to make sure.

4) Peter wants to impress a girl he just started dating. Before planning the perfect outside dinner, he looks up the weather so he can see if it is suitable.

5) James and his kids want to play in the snow. Although, when it's too cold, it's no fun. James checks the weather to see if it's perfect weather for building snowmen.
