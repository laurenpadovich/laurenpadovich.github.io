Repitition

Dr Pepper

drpepper.com

Repitition is strongly exemplified on this page. These kinds of brands use logos to facilitate familiarity with their company. This website shows how much dependence they have on the logo and a strong sense of brand identity.

White Space & Clean Design

RedBubble

redbubble.com

The principle of white space and clean design is shown all throughout this site. The layout of elements is organized and pleasing to the eye and there is plenty of white space to keep aspects of the page from being to close together and looking cluttered.

Visual Hierarchy

Kuubix

kuubixenergy.com

Visual hierarchy plays a role in this page in order to lead the viewer to see what the company wants them to see in order of importance. The phrase in bold and large font "Power your world" is most likely what visitors will see first and gives a glimpse of the page's intent. Next, visitors may see the button and will be prompted to go further into the website.
